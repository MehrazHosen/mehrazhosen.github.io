About Me

Hi, I’m Md Mehraz Hosen from Dhaka, Bangladesh. I am a passionate Computer Science graduate who enjoys learning new stuffs about technology and research methodology. I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from the Bangladesh University of Business and Technology (BUBT), where I graduated in December 2023 with a focus on Artificial Intelligence and its applications in healthcare and language analysis.

My undergraduate thesis, titled “ALCNN: Advanced Lightweight Convolutional Neural Network,” focused on XAI for lung cancer and brain tumor classification and localization. This work achieved a remarkable 99.54% accuracy, surpassing models like MobileNetV2, VGG16, and InceptionV3, and was guided by Assistant Professor Khan Md Hasib. My research interests extend to biomedical imaging, Natural Language Processing (NLP), neural symbolic AI, and knowledge graph reasoning.

In addition to healthcare AI, I’ve contributed to Bengali Natural language processing (NLP), developing models like BengaliHateCB and B-BERTa for hate speech detection using Transformers and LLMs. I have also explored AI-driven IoT projects and other interdisciplinary applications of Machine Learning and Deep Learning.

Research Experience

My research interests lie in applying Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Biomedical Image Analysis, and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to address real-world challenges in healthcare, language understanding. I am particularly interested in Explainable AI (XAI), efficient model design, and the application of computational methods in underrepresented domains like Bangla NLP and resource constrained healthcare environments.
